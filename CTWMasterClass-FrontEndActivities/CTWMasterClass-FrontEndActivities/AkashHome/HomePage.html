Speak To Me Series

The Yale School of Art’s Speak to Me series is an online forum with
invited speakers activists, writers, and artists organized with poet,
playwright, and author Claudia Rankine, Leah Mirakhor, Lecturer in
Ethnicity, Race, and Migration, alongside Dean and Professor
Marta Kuzma, in an effort to address the NOW.

The series is open to the public and began June 9, 2020. Learn more here >>
